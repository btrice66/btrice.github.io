Land of Smiles: Bangkok

What to Do & See

As the main metropolis in Thailand, Bangkok offers an abundance of sights and attractions that will sure make your visit worthwhile!

Markets & Shopping:

Damnoen Saduak Floating Market: The most popular floating market in Thailand, full of boats selling food, fruits and other goods.

Chatuchak Weekend Market Is the largest weekend market in Thailand, containing over 15,000 booths selling goods from all over Thailand.

Temples:

Wat Pho: One of the largest temple complexes in the city and famous for its giant reclining Buddha.

Wat Arun: Deemed as one of the most stunning temples in Bangkok due to its riverside location and colorfully decorated spires.

Wat Prakaew: Regarded as the most sacred Buddhist temple in Thailand as it is home to the sacred Emerald Buddha.

Entertainment & Night life:

Muay Thai or Thai boxing match: Lumpinee Stadium, Rajadamnern Stadium, Channel 7 Stadium are the top places to watch these notorious fighting matches.

Khao San Road: A short, wide street filled with hostels, bars, markets, and massage parlors.

Red Light District: The infamous adult-themed district comprised of Soi Cowboy, Nan Plaza and Patong.
